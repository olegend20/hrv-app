Privacy Policy for HRV Optimizer

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

HRV Optimizer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • User Profile: Age and gender (collected during onboarding)
  • Habit Data: Daily habits you track in the app (sleep, exercise, stress, diet, etc.)
  • Goals: Personal health goals you set within the app

Data from WHOOP

When you connect your WHOOP account, we collect:

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV) readings
  • Resting heart rate
  • Recovery scores
  • Sleep data
  • Workout data
  • Dates associated with physiological cycles

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your HRV trends and statistics
  • Analyze correlations between your habits and HRV
  • Generate personalized recommendations
  • Track your progress toward health goals
  • Provide achievements and motivation

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not:

  • Store your data on external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use your data for advertising

The only external connection is to WHOOP's API to fetch your physiological data, which is transmitted securely using HTTPS.

WHOOP Integration

When you connect to WHOOP:

  • You authenticate directly with WHOOP using OAuth 2.0
  • We only request read-only access to your recovery, cycles, sleep, and workout data
  • Your WHOOP credentials are never stored in our app
  • You can disconnect your WHOOP account at any time from the Profile screen

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Clear all your data from the Profile screen
  • Export: Export your data (CSV format available)
  • Disconnect: Remove WHOOP integration at any time

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete it through the app settings
  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app's data through device settings

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Local data encryption using AsyncStorage
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all API communications
  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • No plaintext storage of sensitive information

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with:

  • WHOOP API: To fetch your physiological data (Privacy Policy: https://www.whoop.com/privacy-policy/)

We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

HRV Optimizer is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
